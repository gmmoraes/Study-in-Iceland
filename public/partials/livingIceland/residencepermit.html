All foreign citizens (except Nordic) coming to Iceland to study need a residence permit if staying longer than three months in Iceland. In order to apply for the resident permit, the applicant must be registered as a full time student at a university, vocational school, or other studies meeting university admission.

EEA/EFTA citizens can apply after their arrival. Use the A-262 form in order to register for a period exceeding 3 months. see further: http:ww.skra.is//english/forms

Inform the National Registry of your departure

Before leaving Iceland all international students must turn in a Notification of Change of Address to the National Registry. Notification of change of address can be delivered electronically. Use form A-255. The form should be downloaded (do not open the form in a browser). Then save the completed document to your computer. Click "Netskil" for electronic delivery. You can find this form here http://www.skra.is/english/forms/

If the form is not delivered electronically, it must be signed and posted to:
